Our Services

The stages our products undergo before the finish line.

Discovery

Drug candidates, discovered in academic and pharmaceutical/biotech research labs, are tested for their interaction with the drug Up to 5,000 to 10,000 molecules for each potential drug candidate are subjected to a rigorous screening process.

Product Characterization

When the candidate molecule shows promise as a therapeutic, it must be characterized—the molecule’s size, shape, strengths and weaknesses, preferred conditions for maintaining function, toxicity, bioactivity, and bioavailability must be determined.

Formulation, Packaging Development

Drug developers must devise a formulation that ensures proper drug delivery parameters. Our Scientists determine the drug’s stability—in the formulation itself, and for all the parameters involved with storage and shipment, such as heat, light, and time.

Pharmacokinetics And Drug Disposition

Our formulation scientists use Pharmacokinetic (PK) and ADME (Absorption/Distribution/Metabolism/Excretion) method to provide useful feedback for parameters such as AUC (area under the curve), Cmax (maximum concentration of the drug in the blood)

Preclinical Toxicology Testing and IND Application

Preclinical testing analyzes the bioactivity, safety, and efficacy of the formulated drug product. We follow these stages of preclinical toxicology testing: Acute, Repeated Dose, Genetic Toxicity, Reproductive Toxicity, Carcinogenicity, Toxicokinetic

Bioanalytical Testing

Bioanalytical laboratory work and bioanalytical method development support most of the other activities in the drug development process. The bioanalytical work is key to the proper characterization of the molecule. Bioanalytical work is so important .

Clinical Trials

The Bioanalytical Team at Gridiron supports clinical trials. Our Clinical studies are grouped according to their objective into three types or phases: Phase I (Human Pharmacology), Phase II (Therapeutic Exploratory), Phase III (Therapeutic Confirmatory).

Certification and Registration

Our Managers help our clients in the processing the certificate of pharmaceutical product, we don't stop there we help even the distributors of the product in the process of registration, licensing and authorisation, we are with you till the end.

Branding, Design and Distribution

We have experts in design, brand and distribution; our team will give your product that looks that befits its quality. Branding and design go a long way in product marketing and sales. We also help our clients in the distribution for their product .

Welcome To VETIS ANIMAL HEALTH

VETIS ANIMAL HEALTH. founded in 1999, is Togo's leading manufacturer and exporter of chemical and pharmaceutical products that includes nutritional products, intermediates, bulk drugs, veterinary products, sweeteners, quinolines, triazole and selenium products. The spectacular growth of VETIS ANIMAL HEALTH over the years, in the domestic as well as the overseas market, is attributed to:

  • Our work ethics
  • Visionary leadership style
  • Strong focus on R&D
  • State-of-art Facilities
  • Customer-friendly Approach

We have a wide range of products for you to choose from, also we can take up new projects from R&D to marketing for you.
